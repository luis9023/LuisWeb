Luis E. Bilbraut

EMAIL-ME


Born on February 23, 1990 in Humacao, P.R.
Raised at Maunabo, P.R.
High School: Alfonso Casta Martinez in Maunabo, P.R.
Currently a Computer Engineering senior student at Universidad Del Turabo in Gurabo, P.R.

I am a super friendly person and something of a dreamer. I love to accomplish my dreams. Growing up with an awesome family, helping me make the good decisions in life.

One of my main dreams was to become a pilot. I will never forget the first time I got in an airplane in summer of 1995. By the age of 18 I obtained my private pilot license. It was one of the best days of my life.

I started college in 2009. I enjoy working with computers and programming. That is why I chose Computer Engineering. It is an exciting major. Lots of challenges, but in the end it's worth it.

In 2010 I became a member of the CCCE (Caribbean Computer Center for Excellence). Thanks to this alliance I've been able to provide some computer workshops at different high schools around Puerto Rico and the U.S. Virgin Islands.
