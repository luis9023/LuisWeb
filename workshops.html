February 22, 2013

Computer Day and Computer Olympiad at Colegio Nuestra Señora del Carmen, San Juan, Puerto Rico.

Scratch

Forty (40) high school students participated in the workshop. Luis Bilbraut, a CCCE Scholar, offered and Introduction to Computer Programing using SCRATCH workshop, along with Tomas Yan Melo, the CCCE Coordinator. After the workshop a Computer Olympiad was held under the guidance of Luis Bilbraut, CCCE Scholar; Natalia Santiago, UMET Undergraduate Student; and Tomas Yan Melo, CCCE Coordinator. The top three scores were Cilianelle Nieves (third place), Patricia Laracuente (second place), and Adriana M. Ortiz (first place). See the highlights!
